Key Rules
🥩
Protein First
Hit 2g per kg of bodyweight every day — training day or not. If you're falling short, add a protein shake to breakfast.
🏋️
Train Day Calories
Eat more on lifting and Hyrox days — bigger breakfast, generous lunch. Don't undereat when you're training hard.
💧
Between Meals
Black coffee, water, and sparkling water are your best friends. They suppress appetite without adding calories.
😴
Rest Days
Eat a little less naturally on rest days — your hunger will guide you. Don't force it either way.
8-Week Progression

Your roadmap from day one to peak week.

Weeks 1–2
Learn the erg stroke, establish lifting baseline, survive Hyrox
Weeks 3–4
Add weight to lifts weekly, push erg intervals harder
Weeks 5–6
Increase interval volume, hit PRs on main lifts
Weeks 7–8
Peak then deload — drop volume 40%, keep intensity
Hyrox Synergy Tips

🚣 Ski Erg — Your erg sessions directly improve this station. Same muscles, same rhythm.


🛷 Sled Push/Pull — Gets easier as your deadlift goes up. Don't skip Monday's leg work.


⚡ Manage fatigue honestly. Six days is a lot. If Thursday or Friday feels empty, make the erg session purely easy — no intervals. Listen to your body week to week.


😴 Sleep and protein become even more critical at 6 days. Hit your protein target every day, not just training days.

Erg goal: drop your 500m split by 1–2 seconds every few weeks. Lifting goal: add 2.5kg to main lifts each week. Small, consistent progress beats big leaps.

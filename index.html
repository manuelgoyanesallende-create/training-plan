Calories By Day Type
🏋️
Lifting Days (Tue, Thu)
Slight calorie deficit. Protein stays at 155g. Moderate carbs at both meals. These sessions build muscle — don't undereat.
🚣
Erg Day (Fri)
Close to maintenance. Higher carbs at breakfast to fuel the session. If doing hard intervals, eat a bigger lunch too.
🔥
Hyrox Days (Mon, Wed, Sat)
At or slightly above maintenance. Biggest breakfast of the week — carbs and protein both up. Three classes a week burns serious calories.
🏃
Run Day (Sun)
Close to maintenance. Good carbs at breakfast before the run. As your long runs grow past 60 min, treat Sunday like a Hyrox day for calories.
Key Rules
🥩
Protein Every Day
155g daily is non-negotiable — training day or not. Split roughly 80g breakfast, 75g lunch. Greek yogurt and eggs at breakfast get you there fast.
😴
Sleep = Gains
At 7 training days, sleep is as important as the sessions themselves. 7–9 hours. Muscle is built at rest, not in the gym.
16-Week Progression

Four phases across 16 weeks — from foundation to peak performance.

Phase 1 — Weeks 1–4
Foundation. Learn erg technique, establish lifting baseline, get used to 6 days. Don't push intensity yet.
Phase 2 — Weeks 5–8
Build. Add 2.5kg to main lifts weekly. Push erg intervals harder. Start tracking 500m split times.
Phase 3 — Weeks 9–12
Strength peak. Hit PRs on cable rows, leg press, overhead press. Add interval reps on the erg. Hyrox times improving.
Phase 4 — Weeks 13–16
Peak then deload. Weeks 13–15 push everything. Week 16: drop volume 40%, keep intensity. Reassess and reset.
Hyrox Synergy Tips

🚣 Ski Erg — Tuesday and Friday erg sessions directly build this station. Same lat and back engagement, same rhythm. You'll feel it improving week by week.


🛷 Sled Push/Pull — Gets easier as your Sunday leg press and leg curl numbers go up. Don't skip legs.


💪 Farmers Carry & Sandbag — Monday pull session builds the grip and back endurance these stations demand. Face pulls keep your shoulders healthy under load.


⚡ Manage fatigue across the week. Wednesday Hyrox sits between Monday pull and Thursday push — arrive fresh. Friday erg is your last hard session before Saturday Hyrox — if your legs are empty, do easy steady state instead of intervals.


😴 Sleep and protein are your recovery tools at 6 days. 155g protein daily, 7–9 hours sleep. These matter more than any individual exercise swap.

Erg goal: drop your 500m split by 1–2 seconds every 2 weeks. Lifting goal: add 2.5kg to cable rows, leg press and overhead press each week during Phase 2. Small consistent progress over 16 weeks compounds massively.
